Submission Guidelines

We invite writers, researchers, academicians, practitioners, and enthusiasts to contribute original and impactful content. Before submitting, kindly review the following guidelines to ensure your work aligns with our objectives and standards.

General Submission Categories

We welcome submissions under the following categories:

1. General Articles

Focused on delivering insightful perspectives and practical knowledge across disciplines such as agriculture, science & technology, health, environment, and social issues. Word Limit: 1,500 to 3,000 words
Format: Calibri, 12 pt, single-spaced
Structure:

  • Author(s) and Affiliation(s)
  • Abstract (150–200 words)
  • Keywords (up to 5)
  • Introduction
  • Main Content (divided into sections with headings)
  • Conclusion/Call to Action
  • References (if applicable)

    • 2. Short Reports & Communications

    Concise updates, innovative approaches, or findings in any relevant field.
    Word Limit: 800 to 1,000 words
    Format: Times New Roman, 12 pt, single-spaced

    3. Tips and Insights

    Practical tips, expert advice, or industry best practices aimed at empowering readers with
    actionable knowledge.
    Word Limit: 800–1,000 words

    4. Recent Advancements

    Updates on groundbreaking developments or emerging trends in science, technology,
    agriculture, or societal impact.
    Word Limit: 1,000–2,000 words

    5. Creative Contributions

    Includes essays, poetry, opinion pieces, or stories that reflect the ethos of Uday Bharat Magazine.
    Word Limit: 800–1,500 words

    Formatting Requirements

    File Format: Submissions should be in MS Word (.doc or .docx).

    Page Margins: 1-inch margin on all sides.

    Language: Submissions must be in English. Ensure that the language is clear and professional.

    Images & Graphics: Include high-resolution images or diagrams, if applicable. Attach them
    separately in .jpg or .png format and indicate their placement within the text.

    Submission Process

    Step 1: Review Guidelines
    Ensure that your submission adheres to the formatting and word count requirements mentioned above.

    Step 2: Fill Out the Submission Form
    Click here to fill out the submission form. Provide accurate details such as your name, affiliation, contact information, and submission title.

    Step 3: Upload Your Work
    Attach your article or report in MS Word format. If your submission includes images or supplementary materials, upload them separately in the designated section of the form.

    Step 4After submitting, you will receive a confirmation email with a reference number for your submission.
    Please retain this email for future correspondence.

    Important Notes

    1. Submissions must be original and unpublished. Plagiarized content will be rejected immediately.
    2. All submissions will undergo editorial review. Acceptance is subject to the discretion of the editorial board.
    3. Authors may be asked to revise their submissions before final acceptance.
    4. By submitting your work, you grant Uday Bharat Magazine the right to publish it in print or online formats.
